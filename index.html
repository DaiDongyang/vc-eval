Voice Conversion Evaluation

Thanks for doing this subjective evalation test! This is a subjective evaluation on voice conversion. You are supposed to evaluate the utterances converted by system1 and system2, while the source speech and reference speech are ground truths from source and target speakers respectively. Please fill in the Excel form attached to this web page.

For naturalness mos evaluation, you should focus on the naturalness of the utterance from system1 and system2 and pay no attention to the speaker similarity with the reference speech. Score the utterances on a scale from 1 (completely unnatural) to 5 (completely natural).

For similarity evaluation, you should focus on the speaker similarity of the utterances to the reference speech and pay no attention to the naturalness. Choose system whose converted speech is more similar to the reference speech, 1 for system1, 2 for system2, NP for no preference.

Attention! The reference speech's linguistic content is not necessarily the same as the converted speech!

Please send the filled Excel form to me via wechat or Email. Thanks again!
